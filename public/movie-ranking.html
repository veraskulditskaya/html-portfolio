The Best Movies According to Vera

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dexter

A thrilling and dark series. Dexter’s unique moral code and double life as a blood-spatter analyst and serial killer keep me on edge.

Misfits

Superpowers with a twist! This show is a crazy ride with humor, dark moments, and unexpected heroes. I love the creative storylines.

HPI: Haut Potentiel Intellectuel

A hilarious French crime-comedy that follows Morgane Alvaro, a loud and witty woman with a 160 IQ who solves mysteries despite her chaotic personal life.
Her knack for solving the toughest cases lands her an unlikely job with the Lille police. It’s the perfect blend of comedy and crime.
